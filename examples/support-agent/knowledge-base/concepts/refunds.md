# Refunds

## Eligibility

Refunds are available within 14 days of a charge. After 14 days, we offer
account credit instead. Enterprise plans have custom refund terms in their
contract.

## How to Request

1. Open a support ticket with subject line "Refund Request"
2. Include the invoice number (found in [Billing](billing.md) dashboard)
3. Our team reviews within 2 business days

## Processing Time

- Credit card refunds: 5–10 business days
- ACH refunds: 10–15 business days
- Account credits: applied immediately

## Exceptions

Refunds are not available for usage-based overages or add-on purchases
activated more than 48 hours ago.
