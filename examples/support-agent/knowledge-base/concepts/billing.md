# Billing

All Acme plans are billed monthly on the anniversary of the account creation date.

## Payment Methods

We accept Visa, Mastercard, and ACH bank transfers. Payment methods can be
updated in **Settings → Billing → Payment Methods**.

## Invoices

Invoices are generated 3 days before the billing date and emailed to the
account owner. Past invoices are available in the billing dashboard.

## Failed Payments

If a payment fails, we retry twice over 7 days. After the third failure the
account is downgraded to the free tier. See [troubleshooting](troubleshooting.md)
for common payment failure causes.
